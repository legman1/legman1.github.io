duofoodgo

A project I did with 4 other friends for the SYNCSHACK 2021 hackathon. Duofoodgo is a mobile app which encourages users to go outside and explore restaurants in a pokemon go-like fashion.

This would be done by rewarding a virtual pet in the app everytime they dined at a location. This project became a finalist in the hackathon.
